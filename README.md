### **Amazon Global Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI**

Developed an interactive **Amazon Global Sales Performance Dashboard** in **Power BI** to analyze sales performance across multiple business dimensions. The dashboard provides insights into **total sales, profit, profit margin, orders, quantity sold, customer type, region, country, product category, and monthly sales trends**. Implemented dynamic filters (Year, Country, Month, and Customer Type) to enable interactive data exploration and decision-making. Used **Power Query** for data cleaning and transformation, created **DAX measures** for KPI calculations, and designed visually appealing reports with maps, charts, and KPI cards. This dashboard helps identify sales trends, top-performing products, regional performance, and customer purchasing patterns, supporting data-driven business decisions.
